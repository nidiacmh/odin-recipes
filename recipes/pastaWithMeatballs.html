"Lasagna"

An Italian-American favorite that combines tender pasta with juicy meatballs simmered in a rich tomato sauce. Topped with Parmesan cheese, it’s a comforting and satisfying dish loved by many.

Ingredients

For the Meatballs

  1. 1 lb (500 g) ground beef (or a mix of beef and pork)
  2. 1 egg
  3. ½ cup breadcrumbs
  4. ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  5. 2 garlic cloves, minced
  6. 1 teaspoon salt
  7. ½ teaspoon black pepper
  8. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  9. 1 teaspoon dried or fresh parsley
  10. Olive oil, for frying

For the Tomato Sauce

  1. 1 tablespoon olive oil
  2. 1 small onion, chopped
  3. 3 garlic cloves, minced
  4. 1 can (28 oz or 800 g) crushed tomatoes
  5. 1 teaspoon sugar
  6. Salt and pepper to taste
  7. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  8. 1 bay leaf
  9. Fresh basil (optional)

For the Pasta

  1. 400 g (about 14 oz) spaghetti or fettuccine
  2. 400 g (about 14 oz) spaghetti or fettuccine

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix the ground meat, egg, breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, garlic, salt, pepper, oregano, and parsley until well combined.
  2. Shape the mixture into small balls, about 1 tablespoon each, and place them on a plate.
  3. Heat some olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Add the meatballs and cook until browned on all sides, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the meatballs from the skillet and set aside.
  5. In the same skillet, add a little more olive oil if needed and sauté the onion until translucent, about 3-4 minutes.
  6. Add the garlic and cook for another minute, stirring to prevent burning.
  7. Add the crushed tomatoes, sugar, salt, pepper, oregano, and bay leaf. Let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  8. Place the meatballs in the sauce and let them cook together for 10-15 minutes, until the meatballs are fully cooked and the flavors blend.
  9. In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook the pasta according to package instructions until al dente.
  10. Drain the pasta and toss it with a little of the sauce to prevent sticking.
  11. Serve the pasta in bowls, place some meatballs on top, and add extra sauce if desired. Sprinkle with grated Parmesan and fresh basil for garnish.
