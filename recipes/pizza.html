"Pizza"

A classic Italian dish featuring a thin or thick crust topped with savory tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and various toppings like pepperoni, mushrooms, and fresh vegetables. It’s a versatile, crowd-pleasing meal that’s perfect for sharing.

Ingredients

For the Dough

  1. 2 ¼ teaspoons active dry yeast (1 packet)
  2. 1 ½ cups warm water (110°F or 45°C)
  3. 3 ½ - 4 cups all-purpose flour
  4. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  5. 2 teaspoons salt
  6. 1 teaspoon sugar

For the Sauce

  1. 1 cup tomato sauce
  2. 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  3. 1 teaspoon dried basil
  4. 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  5. Salt and pepper to taste

For the Toppings

  1. 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  2. Any desired toppings (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, onions, olives, etc.)

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, dissolve the yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 5-10 minutes, until it becomes foamy.
  2. Add olive oil, salt, and 3 ½ cups of flour to the yeast mixture. Mix until a dough forms.
  3. Transfer the dough to a floured surface and knead for 6-8 minutes, adding more flour if necessary, until the dough is smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1-1 ½ hours, or until it doubles in size.
  5. n a small bowl, combine the tomato sauce, oregano, basil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Stir well and set aside.

  6. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  7. Punch down the risen dough to release air bubbles, then divide it in half if you want two smaller pizzas.
  8. or stretch the dough into a circle or rectangle (about 12 inches wide if using the whole dough), depending on your baking sheet or pizza stone.
  9. Place the dough on a lightly greased baking sheet or preheated pizza stone.
  10. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  11. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce, then add your desired toppings.
  12. for 10-12 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  13. Let the pizza cool for a couple of minutes, then slice and enjoy!
